ASME VIII-2 Part 5 Analysis Methods

Method 1: Elastic Stress Analysis

ASME Reference: VIII-2 Part 5.2.2

Purpose: Stress categorization and linearization

  • Membrane stress (Pm) evaluation
  • Membrane + bending (PL + Pb)
  • Stress linearization paths
  • Allowable stress checks

Method 2: Limit-Load Analysis

ASME Reference: VIII-2 Part 5.2.3

Purpose: Plastic collapse prevention

  • Small displacement theory
  • Perfectly plastic material
  • Load multiplier calculation
  • Collapse load determination

Method 3: Elastic-Plastic Analysis

ASME Reference: VIII-2 Part 5.2.4

Purpose: Strain limits and ratcheting

  • True stress-strain curve
  • Cyclic loading simulation
  • Strain accumulation check
  • Ratcheting assessment

Analysis Approach for This Study

All three methods were applied to critical locations:

  • Nozzle-to-shell junction: Elastic stress analysis with stress linearization
  • Head knuckle region: Limit-load analysis for collapse prevention
  • Saddle support: Elastic-plastic analysis for cyclic loading
